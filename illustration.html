Below are some art I did for one of my dnd characters, "Null"

The general background is that my character, a changeling named Kass, is a killer and thief in the backstory of one of my party members' characters, Olwe (unbeknownst to both that character and the player). Afterwards, Olwe enlisted the help of a tiefling rogue named Ralmir (who goes by Null to hide his identity) to help him find Kass. Kass stalked Olwe and Ralmir before killing Ralmir one night and transforming into him, stealing his identity and going with Olwe to lead him astray, all while collecting the recurring payment Olwe gave Ralmir for his help.
